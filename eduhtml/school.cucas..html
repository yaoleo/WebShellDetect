CUCAS and China Universities

Why Apply China Universities Through CUCAS

We care your dream of study in China more than others.

Authorized Service

CUCAS, known as China's University and College Admission System, is an official online portal for international students applying to China's universities. It is jointly operated by CEAIE and Chiwest Ltd. a China’s leading IT company.

CUCAS provides most complete and timely information of China's Universities as well as one-stop online application service for international students who want to study in China. By 2015, CUCAS has built solid cooperation with 300+ top China universities including famous ones like Tsinghua University, Beijing Language and Culture University, Dalian Medical University Zhejiang University and etc. With years of good cooperation, we help thousands of students enter the gate of China universities every year smoothly.

No. 1 in Industry

CUCAS cares each international student’s dream of study in China. We believe that our care to others dream could help us achieve ours. With this faith in mind, CUCAS is always committed to providing top class services in the whole process of study in China.

Before you come to China, we provide professional and free consulting service, and we reply email within 24 hours. We also provide more information such as ranking and reviews to help you on choosing university.

When start applying on CUCAS, you will feel the simplicity and delightness our online application service brings to you. Our experienced team helps you review each document and information you provide until you are perfectly ready for admission.

We also provide convenient application process tracking service to let you clearly know which step you are in and what you need to do next.

When you got the admission, we mail your documents via DHL for free for the first time, and we will also provide a surprising gift for you in the package for admission documents.

With all the most elaborated service we provide, we have become No.1 in this industry. No matter in Alexa traffic rank, numbers of applicants, social network fans, customer satisfaction or reputation in both China universities and foreign students.

Reliability and Security

We are the only online application system backed by Chinese universities and the organization which is authorized to use the official ‘edu.cn’ domain. We are also recommended by many local and international official organizations, such as: French Embassy China Website, UK Council for International Exchange and Education Network Australia.

Affordability

CUCAS is the most cost-effective way to apply for Chinese universities. The service fee for each application is only $50, which covers all the service we provide in application process including DHL service for mailing admission documents to your country.

CUCAS Internationalization

Go international, go big! CUCAS has also built cooperation with over one hundred agencies around the world, including America, UK, France, India, Tanzania and South Africa, which enable students in different countries access CUCAS service easily. In the near future, CUCAS will pay continuous effort to speed up the pace of internationalization to reach more students in the world.

Apply through CUCAS, make your dream come true!
