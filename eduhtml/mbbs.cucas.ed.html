MBBS Scholarship

Chinese University Scholarships
To encourage and reward outstanding international students who study English medium MBBS program at their respective universities, most of the universities represented by CUCAS have set up an "Excellent Scholarship for International Students".

Learn More

Local Government Scholarships
China's provincial and municipal governments offer scholarships for international students pursuing degree education including English medium MBBS program in local universities. Applicants should submit their scholarship applications directly to the university.
