Recently, Ms. Men Yanping, Deputy Secretary of SMU Party Committee and Secretary of Commission for Discipline Inspection was invited to visit WMU in Malmo, Sweden. During the visit, she met with Dr. Cleopatra Doumbia-Henry, President of WMU, Prof. Ma Shuo, Vice President of WMU and other representatives. Ms. Men Yanping said WMU and SMU had maintained close exchanges and cooperation for more than two decades. The project International Transport and Logistics Management jointly organized by the two universities have been successfully held for more than 10 years. She hoped that the two sides could make further cooperation to contribute to the development of maritime and shipping industry. Dr. Cleopatra Doumbia-Henry introduced the development history and the running situation of WMU, as well as the opportunities for WMU after moving to the new site. During September 18-21, Prof. Huang Youfang was invited to attend Smart Rivers Conference 2017 held in Pittsburgh in United States. The Conference was hosted by PIANC and organized by PIANC USA. Prof. Ge Yingen, Dean of College of Transport and Communications and Dr. Hu Jiankun from Logistics Research Center attended the meeting together. During the Conference, Prof. Huang Youfang met with Mr. Jim McCarville, Chairman of the Organizing Committee, Mr. Geert Van Cappellen, Secretary-General of PIANC and other two vice-presidents of PIANC. PIANC proposed that Shanghai Maritime Univeristy should bid for Smart Rivers Conference 2021. In this regard, President Huang Youfang said SMU is willing to actively strive for and will report to the Ministry of Transport and the relevant authorities in Shanghai, preparing the bid for Smart Rivers Conference 2021.
